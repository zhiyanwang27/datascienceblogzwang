Introduction to Hypothesis Testing and the Credibility Issues in Psychology Studies

This blog is Introduction to Hypothesis Testing and the Credibility Issues in Psychology Studies. I will introduce the concept of hypothesis testing with real-life examples.

In psychology researches, hypothesis testing is widely used. Recently, there has been great concerns about the replicability of previous findings. A larger number of these credibility concerns have been related to the misuse of hypothesis testing. I will discuss the hypothesis testing and the credibility issues.
